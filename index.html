// About Us

DC415 is a group created to bring together people who share a passion for cybersecurity and a curiosity about how things work. We are a community of security researchers, hackers, engineers, and curious minds who gather to learn, share, and push the boundaries of what's possible in cybersecurity.

Our meetings are open to everyone — from seasoned professionals to complete beginners. Whether you've been rooting boxes since the BBS days or just started your first CTF, you belong here. No elitism, no gatekeeping, just people who love the craft.

Each meetup features talks, demos, and open discussions covering everything from offensive security and reverse engineering to privacy, policy, and the ethics of hacking. The format is informal and driven by the community itself.
